Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I adopt a pet? +
Visit our Available Pets page, choose a pet, and fill out the adoption form. Our team will contact you shortly.
2. What documents do I need to adopt? +
A valid photo ID, proof of address, and basic information about your living situation are required.
3. Is there an adoption fee? +
Adoption is free. Donations are appreciated to help rescue more animals.
4. Can I return a pet if it doesn’t work out? +
We encourage patience and bonding, but if serious issues arise, you can safely return the pet to our shelter.
5. Do you have pets suitable for apartments? +
Yes, we have cats and small dogs that adapt well to apartment living.
